5th Grade Afaan Oromoo as a Second language

If you want to learn Afaan Oromoo app is for you. This application is designed to help a number of people who want to study and improve their Afaan Oromoo speaking skills using these pocket book android application designed for Grade 5th student.

The application contents are designed and arranged according to the chapter from the first chapter which is chapter 01 to chapter 11 means from the basic to advanced in the textbook. All the chapters are listed as follows

  • Greeting Each Other
  • The Alphabet
  • Afaan Oromoo letter sounds: a/aa to ja/jaa
  • Sounds of Afaan Oromoo Language
  • Sounds of Afaan Oromoo Language
  • Simple and geminated sounds in Afaan oromoo
  • Sound consonant-vowel and glottal
  • Family
  • My School
  • Food
  • The Season
